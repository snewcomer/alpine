Make more from your cheese

Who We Are

Alpine Slicing and Cheese Conversion has been specializing in cheese conversion since 2005. Our facility is located in the heart of Green County dairy and cheese country Monroe Wisconsin. Alpine Slicing and Cheese Conversion caters to all types of customers both large and small with logistics covering the entire United States.

Our Mission

We exist to add value to the cheese industry by providing safe, consistent and creative converting and packaging solutions.

Services

Cheese Slicing and Converting

The cheese industry in Wisconsin is changing, and so are the needs for cheese converting. Between the Alpine owners, we have over 100 years of experience in the cheese industry, but Alpine prides itself on innovation and ingenuity when working with customers to bring their products to market. We can create retail, foodservice or specialty packaging in a variety of formats and we can slice, stick, or chunk cheese in a wide range of materials. We work almost exclusively with cheesemakers and distributors and can offer a package designed to add value.
